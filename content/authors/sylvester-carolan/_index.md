Sylvester Carolan has spent over three decades solving complex problems at massive scale — from trading platforms handling $50B in daily transactions to AI/ML systems that reduced incidents by 45%. Now he's channeling that experience into civic technology.

As an active contributor to the Kubernetes ecosystem and CNCF, Sylvester brings deep expertise in cloud-native architectures to projects that matter. He currently leads engineering efforts for WeVote, building tools to help voters make informed decisions at the ballot box.

## Areas of Focus
- Cloud-native architecture and infrastructure
- AI/ML for civic applications
- Voter information systems
- Open source civic technology
- Engineering for social good

Sylvester believes that good infrastructure serves people, not the other way around. He writes about the intersection of engineering discipline and civic engagement, sharing lessons learned from decades of building systems that actually ship.
