Restore Democracy is dedicated to providing citizens with clear, factual information about their elected representatives and candidates for office.

We believe an informed electorate is the foundation of a healthy democracy. Our mission is to research and publish nonpartisan analyses of political races across the country, helping voters make educated decisions at the ballot box.

### What We Do

- Research candidates and incumbents at the federal, state, and local level
- Publish analysis of voting records, policy positions, and campaign finance
- Track legislative activity and hold representatives accountable to their constituents

### Who We Are

We are a group of volunteers committed to transparency in government and the preservation of democratic institutions. We have no affiliation with any political party or outside organization.
